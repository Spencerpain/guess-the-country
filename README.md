# Guess the Country

A geography quiz game where a country is highlighted on the world map and you try to name it.

**[Play the game](https://spencerpain.github.io/guess-the-country/)**

## How to play

1. Choose a difficulty and quiz length
2. A country lights up orange on the map — name it in your head
3. Click **Reveal Answer** to see if you were right
4. Mark yourself correct or wrong
5. See your final score and accuracy at the end

## Difficulties

| Difficulty | Countries |
|---|---|
| Easy | 20 large, well-known countries |
| Medium | ~48 common world countries |
| Hard | ~84 countries across all continents |
| Impossible | ~25 tiny & obscure territories |

## Quiz lengths

10 · 15 · 25 · 50 questions
